Admissions Process

We seek students who demonstrate academic potential, character, and a commitment to learning.

1

Inquiry

Submit an inquiry form or attend an open house to learn more about our programs.

2

Application

Complete our online application form and submit required documents.

3

Assessment

Schedule a student assessment and family interview with our admissions team.

4

Decision

Receive your admissions decision within 2-3 weeks of completing all steps.

Application Requirements

Completed Application

Submit the online application form with all required information.

Academic Transcripts

Previous two years of academic records and any standardized test scores.

Recommendations

Two teacher recommendations from current or previous instructors.

Student Essay

Personal statement or essay demonstrating writing skills and personality.
